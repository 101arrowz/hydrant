Firehose does not store any of your data outside of your browser. Data is only transmitted upstream in one of two cases:

  1. Google Analytics is enabled, which sends data on every page load.
  2. When you export to Google Calendar, Firehose sends calendar information to Google to place into your calendar.

No data is transmitted otherwise. That means that our servers do not store your class or calendar information. You can disable Google Analytics without imapcting your use of the site, and if you never export to Google Calendar we never send your data anywhere else.
